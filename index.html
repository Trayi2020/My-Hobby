My Work

TOO LATE TO REGRET Once upon a time there lived a girl named Samaira with her mother and father. She was a brilliant child and well mannered. Her teacher praised her for her sincerity. She always submitted her work on time and was good in extracurricular activities as well . Suddenly, it was reported that a virus outbreak a global health emergency , due to which pandemic was declared. So, the government ordered lock down as a result Samaira was unable to go to school however, started with online classes from home on mobile or laptops. She started exploring more internets and got diverted into online games and videos. In no time she got distracted from her studies and started spending hours and hours on mobile games. She never completed her homework nor concentrated in the class rather ignored teachers. She started lying to her parents when they asked about her studies. She stopped going outside playing with her friends in the neighbour and said them that she has study pressure. Soon pandemic was over and everything was going good. Samaira started going back to her school and realized that she was having problem with her eyes, was unable to see blackboard. Also she drastically failed in her exams. All her years earned reputation in her school became worthless. She realized her mistakes and cried a lot. Her parents supported her and advised her to be attentive and sincere again also to not use mobiles at all.
